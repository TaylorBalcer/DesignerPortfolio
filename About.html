ABOUT ME

Throughout Taylor’s life she has enjoyed making art in many different forms and media. She taught herself HTML and graphic design in 2005 and continued learning about similar subjects throughout her intermediate and high school years. She then spent 8 years in the service industry before deciding to rekindle her passion and further her knowledge of web design and development at Pittsburgh Technical College. Taylor was selected to assist the Digital Marketing Department as a website design/development and UX designer working on PTC's many websites. Through this work she discovered her love for digital marketing, front-end development, UI/UX, and the various aspects of web design. As you browse the site be sure to launch each project and view Taylor’s creativity, talents and passion for web design.
