About Me

Hello! My name is Jamie Begonia and I am an environmental scientist who enjoys growing and foraging plants, flowers, fruits, vegetables, and fungi in my free time. I take you on an adventure to forage fruits and fungi. I also provide information on finding wild edible plants and fungi, tips for storing and regrowing store-bought fruits and vegetables, and a beginner’s guide to getting started!

Here at Jamie’s Hunting Vegetables Blog, you can learn ALL about foraging wild edible plants and mushrooms. Specifically, I forage in Western Pennsylvania, so some of what I find and forage may only be found in certain parts of the United States, but are not limited to Western Pennsylvania or even the East Coast of the United States.

It is recommended that you do your research! If you plan on beginning to forage wild plants and mushrooms you’ve come to the right place to start! I will share my personal journey and knowledge of accessible wild plants and mushrooms and provide links to credible informative articles, videos, or other forms of media to aid you on your foraging adventures.
