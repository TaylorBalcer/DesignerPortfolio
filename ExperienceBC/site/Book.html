Other Ways to Travel

SkyTrain

British Columbia SkyTrain opened 1985 there are now 53 stations throughout the province. Getting around Vancouver is easy, especially when you board the SkyTrain. This elevated, light rapid transit system travels throughout downtown Vancouver and its surrounding municipalities, with a number of notable destinations along the way.
