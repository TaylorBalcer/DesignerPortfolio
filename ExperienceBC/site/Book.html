Other Ways to Travel

SkyTrain

British Columbia's SkyTrain opened in 1985. There are now 53 stations throughout the province. Getting around Vancouver is easy, especially when you board the SkyTrain. This elevated, light rapid transit system travels throughout downtown Vancouver and its surrounding municipalities, with a number of notable destinations along the way. Read this quick guide to start planning a day of sightseeing via SkyTrain.

Ferry

BC Ferries began in 1960 and now there are 25 routes on the West coast of BC with 35 ferries moving more than 22 million passengers every year. The closet terminal to Coal Harbour Spb airport is in Horseshoe Bay, Vancouver. Board the ferry for your adventure to the Sunshine Coast
